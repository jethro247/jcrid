Jethro Clunies-Ross takes a personal approach
to interior design, providing a complete design
service of the highest quality.

Originally trained in fine art and furniture design, Jethro has also worked as a graphic designer and draws on this creative background to inform his current designs.

Avoiding a rigid house style, he fuses contemporary and traditional to create beautiful, flexible interiors
that reflect his clients' personal style, with a modern, refined twist. Intelligent use of space and strong, elegant styling are his trademarks.
Jethro has formed strong relationships with his clients, most commissions generating repeat work or personal referrals. Click here to read testimonials.
